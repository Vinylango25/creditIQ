📚 About First Payment Default (FPD)

FPD occurs when a borrower fails to make their very first scheduled repayment. In mobile lending, FPD is the most critical early warning metric because:

  • It signals the loan was likely fraudulent or severely mis-sold — the borrower never intended to repay
  • It is used as a primary label for model training — FPD-rate reduction is the #1 model success metric
  • It drives collection strategy — FPD accounts receive intensive outreach within 48 hours
  • Industry benchmark: FPD < 5% for healthy mobile lending; >10% triggers risk review
